Open the All_Demos.vi in the Examples folder.
Use the HelpBrowser.vi in the doc folder to view documentation.

How to manually remove the free app from memory:
Double click the home button. A list of recent apps are shown.
Hold on an one of them until they start to wiggle.
Tap the red minus button on VIremoteFree.
